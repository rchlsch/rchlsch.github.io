History About CoffeeStop

CoffeeStop was born from the idea of ​​2 young people who have just entered the business world.
Rachel loves coffee, Moses loves innovation, and they both love traveling.

According to them, spending time chatting with new people while sipping a cup of hot coffee is a very pleasant thing.
For that, they create a new business idea that combines all the things they like.

CoffeeStop's vision is to help coffee farmers in small cities. Coffeestop visited coffee plantations throughout Indonesia, while introducing new types of coffee that they got to the wider community. With the hope that people know that the land that we step on, produces a delicious coffee.

CoffeeStop's journey starts in the city of Bandung. The city where Rachel and Moses planned their ideas. They plan to be able to get around Indonesia, look for coffee beans that have been hidden all this time, and mix them into a delicious cup of coffee called "perfecto".
